Privacy Policy

隐私政策

Last Updated: May 18, 2025

最后更新：2025年5月18日

Diet Scan AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for our nutrition analysis and calorie counting app.

Diet Scan AI（"我们"或"我们的"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策解释了我们的营养分析和卡路里计算应用程序的数据处理实践。

Data Handling Practices

数据处理实践

Local Storage Only

仅本地存储

All data you enter in Diet Scan AI, including health metrics (weight, height, age), fitness goals, and food logs, is stored exclusively on your device.

您在Diet Scan AI中输入的所有数据，包括健康指标（体重、身高、年龄）、健身目标和食物日志，仅存储在您的设备上。

We do not maintain user accounts or store your personal data on any servers.

我们不维护用户账户，也不在任何服务器上存储您的个人数据。

You maintain complete control over your information at all times.

您始终完全控制您的信息。

Food Analysis API Use

食物分析API使用

When you use our app to analyze food items:

当您使用我们的应用程序分析食物项目时：

  • Food item descriptions/names are sent to our third-party nutrition analysis API
  • 食物项目描述/名称会发送到我们的第三方营养分析API
  • No personal identifying information is sent with these food analysis requests
  • 这些食物分析请求不会发送任何个人身份信息
  • The API returns nutritional information which is displayed in the app and stored only on your device
  • API返回的营养信息会显示在应用程序中，并且仅存储在您的设备上
  • The food analysis service may maintain anonymous logs of food queries to improve their service accuracy
  • 食物分析服务可能会维护食物查询的匿名日志，以提高其服务准确性
  • All communication with the API is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • 与API的所有通信都使用行业标准协议进行加密

Required App Store Declarations

应用商店要求声明

In compliance with Apple's Privacy Nutrition Labels requirements, we declare:

为遵守苹果的隐私营养标签要求，我们声明：

  • Data Not Collected: We do not collect any personal information, contact information, identifiers, or health data from your device
  • 不收集数据：我们不从您的设备收集任何个人信息、联系信息、标识符或健康数据
  • Data Used for App Functionality Only: The food data submitted to our API is used solely for the purpose of nutritional analysis and is not linked to your identity
  • 数据仅用于应用功能：提交给我们API的食物数据仅用于营养分析目的，不与您的身份关联
  • No Tracking: We do not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies
  • 无跟踪：我们不会在其他公司拥有的应用程序或网站上跟踪您

Apple Analytics

苹果分析

  • Anonymous usage statistics may be collected by Apple through their standard analytics tools
  • 苹果可能通过其标准分析工具收集匿名使用统计数据
  • This data is anonymized and aggregated by Apple and helps us understand app performance
  • 这些数据由苹果匿名化和汇总，帮助我们了解应用程序性能
  • You can opt out of this data collection through your device settings at any time
  • 您可以随时通过设备设置选择退出此数据收集

Your Rights and Control

您的权利和控制

  • Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all data by simply deleting the app
  • 由于您的所有数据都存储在您的设备本地，您可以通过简单地删除应用程序来删除所有数据
  • No data recovery is possible once the app is deleted, as we don't maintain any backups on servers
  • 一旦删除应用程序，就无法恢复数据，因为我们不在服务器上维护任何备份

Changes to This Policy

本政策的变更

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。我们将通过在此页面上发布新政策并更新"最后更新"日期来通知您任何变更。

联系我们

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@dietscanai.app.

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过support@dietscanai.app与我们联系。
