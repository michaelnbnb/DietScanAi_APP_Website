How Accurate is DietScanAi?

DietScanAi的准确性如何？

DietScanAi's image scanning feature is generally 90% accurate. For foods with hidden ingredients like smoothies or soups, you can use our describe feature to type exactly what you ate. You can also use our barcode scan or food label scan features for packaged foods.

DietScanAi的图像扫描功能通常有90%的准确率。对于含有隐藏成分的食物，如冰沙或汤，您可以使用我们的描述功能来输入您所吃的确切内容。您还可以使用我们的食品标签扫描功能来识别包装食品。

Frequently Asked Questions

常见问题

Is DietScanAi available for Android?

DietScanAi是否有安卓版本？

Currently, DietScanAi is only available for iOS devices. We're working on an Android version that will be released in the future.

目前，DietScanAi仅适用于iOS设备。我们正在开发安卓版本，将在未来发布。

How does DietScanAi calculate the glycemic index?

DietScanAi如何计算血糖指数？

DietScanAi uses a comprehensive database of foods and their glycemic index values. When analyzing your meal, it identifies the components and provides the GI based on established nutritional data.

DietScanAi使用全面的食物数据库及其血糖指数值。在分析您的餐食时，它会识别组成部分并根据已建立的营养数据提供GI值。

Can I track my nutrition history over time?

我可以随时间追踪我的营养历史吗？

Yes, DietScanAi stores your meal data securely, allowing you to review past meals and see trends in your nutrition intake over days, weeks, and months.

是的，DietScanAi安全地存储您的餐食数据，使您能够查看过去的餐食并查看您在数天、数周和数月内的营养摄入趋势。

Is my data secure?

我的数据安全吗？

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. Your personal information and meal data are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.

是的，我们非常重视数据隐私。您的个人信息和餐食数据经过加密并安全存储。未经您的明确同意，我们绝不会与第三方共享您的数据。
