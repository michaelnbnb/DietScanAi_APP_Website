Frequently Asked Questions

常见问题解答

How accurate is DietScanAi's food recognition?

DietScanAi的食物识别准确度如何？

DietScanAi uses advanced AI technology to identify foods with high accuracy. Our system can recognize thousands of common food items and dishes. For best results, take clear photos in good lighting and position the food centrally in the frame.

DietScanAi使用先进的AI技术，能够高精度识别食物。我们的系统可以识别数千种常见食品和菜肴。为获得最佳效果，请在良好的光线下拍摄清晰的照片，并将食物放在画面中央。

Can I track my daily nutrition intake over time?

我可以长期追踪每日营养摄入量吗？

Yes! DietScanAi stores your meal history, allowing you to view daily, weekly, and monthly nutrition trends. You can track calories, macronutrients (protein, carbs, fat), and glycemic index over time to help achieve your health goals.

是的！DietScanAi会存储您的餐食历史记录，让您可以查看每日、每周和每月的营养趋势。您可以长期追踪卡路里、宏量营养素（蛋白质、碳水化合物、脂肪）和血糖指数，帮助您实现健康目标。

Is my data secure and private?

我的数据安全和隐私是否有保障？

Absolutely. We take data privacy very seriously. Your food photos and nutrition data are stored securely and never shared with third parties without your consent. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

当然。我们非常重视数据隐私。您的食物照片和营养数据被安全存储，未经您的同意，绝不会与第三方共享。有关更多详情，请查看我们的隐私政策。

How do I get the most accurate results?

如何获得最准确的结果？

For best results: 1) Take photos in good lighting, 2) Position food clearly in the frame, 3) Try to separate different food items when possible, and 4) Take photos from above for flat dishes and from a 45-degree angle for dishes with height.

为获得最佳结果：1）在良好的光线下拍照，2）将食物清晰地放在画面中，3）尽可能将不同的食物分开，4）对于平面菜肴从上方拍摄，对于有高度的菜肴从45度角拍摄。

Can I manually add or edit food items?

我可以手动添加或编辑食物项目吗？

Yes, DietScanAi allows you to manually add food items or edit the AI's recognition results. This is useful for custom recipes, rare foods, or when you want to adjust portion sizes.

是的，DietScanAi允许您手动添加食物项目或编辑AI的识别结果。这对于自定义食谱、罕见食物或当您想调整份量大小时非常有用。

Troubleshooting

故障排除

The app is not recognizing my food correctly

应用无法正确识别我的食物

Try taking the photo in better lighting, make sure the food is clearly visible, and try different angles. You can also manually edit the recognition results if needed.

尝试在更好的光线下拍照，确保食物清晰可见，并尝试不同的角度。如有需要，您也可以手动编辑识别结果。

The app crashes when I try to take a photo

当我尝试拍照时应用崩溃

Please ensure your iOS is updated to the latest version. Also, check if you've granted camera permissions to the app. If the problem persists, try restarting your device or reinstalling the app.

请确保您的iOS已更新到最新版本。另外，检查您是否已授予应用相机权限。如果问题仍然存在，请尝试重启设备或重新安装应用。
